Inventory Management

Comprehensive stock management with multi-warehouse support and automated reordering.

Key Features

  • Product Management: Track products with SKUs, variants, and pricing
  • Warehouse Management: Multiple locations with stock transfers
  • Real-Time Tracking: Automatic stock updates from sales and purchases
  • Automated Reordering: Set minimum levels for automatic PO generation
  • Stock Valuation: FIFO, LIFO, and weighted average methods

Stock Tracking

Monitor inventory across all warehouses with real-time updates:

  • Stock level reports
  • Stock movement history
  • Aging inventory analysis
  • Dead stock identification

Tip: Set reorder points 2 weeks before you typically run out to maintain optimal stock levels.
