Getting Started with DreamBiz

Comprehensive AI-powered accounting platform designed to streamline your business financial management.

Welcome to DreamBiz

DreamBiz is a comprehensive, AI-powered accounting platform designed to streamline your business financial management. This guide will help you get started with the essential features and set up your account.

Tip: Take your time exploring each module. DreamBiz is designed to be intuitive and user-friendly.

Quick Setup Guide

Step 1: Create Your Company Profile

Navigate to Accounts → Company Settings to set up your company information:

  • Company name and registration details
  • Business address and contact information
  • Tax identification numbers
  • Fiscal year settings
  • Currency preferences

Step 2: Configure Chart of Accounts

Set up your chart of accounts to match your business structure:

  • Assets (Current, Fixed, Intangible)
  • Liabilities (Current, Long-term)
  • Equity accounts
  • Revenue accounts
  • Expense categories

Step 3: Add Your Team

Invite team members and set up user roles in HR → Employee Management:

  • Admin: Full system access
  • Accountant: Financial records access
  • Manager: Department-specific access
  • Employee: Limited access to personal data

Step 4: Configure Bank Accounts

Add your business bank accounts for reconciliation:

  • Bank name and account number
  • Opening balance
  • Account currency
  • Bank statement import settings

Step 5: Import Existing Data (Optional)

Import data from your previous accounting system:

  • Customer and vendor lists
  • Product/service catalog
  • Opening balances
  • Historical transactions (if needed)
