Dashboard

Your command center for monitoring business performance with real-time insights into key financial metrics and operational KPIs.

Overview

The Dashboard is your command center for monitoring your business performance at a glance. It provides real-time insights into key financial metrics and operational KPIs.

Key Metrics Displayed

Revenue

  • Total revenue for current period
  • Comparison with previous period
  • Revenue trend chart
  • Revenue by category breakdown

Expenses

  • Total expenses for current period
  • Top expense categories
  • Expense trend analysis
  • Budget vs actual comparison

Profit/Loss

  • Net profit/loss calculation
  • Profit margin percentage
  • Period-over-period comparison
  • Profitability trend chart

Cash Flow

  • Current cash position
  • Cash inflow and outflow
  • Projected cash flow for next 30 days
  • Low balance alerts

Outstanding Invoices

  • Total accounts receivable
  • Overdue invoices count and amount
  • Average days to payment
  • Top customers by outstanding balance

Quick Actions

From the dashboard, you can quickly access common tasks:

Create Invoice

Generate a new invoice for customers

Record Expense

Add a new expense entry

Bank Reconciliation

Match transactions with bank statements

View Reports

Access financial reports

Customization

You can customize your dashboard to show the metrics most important to your business:

  • Rearrange widget positions by dragging and dropping
  • Show/hide specific widgets based on your preferences
  • Set custom date ranges for metrics
  • Choose between chart types (line, bar, pie)
  • Save multiple dashboard layouts
